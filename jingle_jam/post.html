What is Jingle Jam?

Jingle Jam is an annual charity event organized by the Yogscast, bringing together gamers, streamers, and fans to raise money for incredible charities. It takes place each December and features livestreams, challenges, and a special charity bundle with amazing games.

How Can You Get Involved?

There are plenty of ways to participate:

  • Watch and Donate: Tune into the streams here, enjoy the content, and donate to support the event's chosen charities.
  • Get the Bundle: For donation of $, you can receive a bundle of great games while contributing to charity. See here to view the game list.
  • Spread the Word: Share the event with your friends and family to help make an even bigger impact!
  • Check the streamers milestones to see the fun additions to the stream.
